04 — Competitive Analysis

Every Competitor
Has a Fatal Flaw.

Yes — Jotform has 40+ payment gateways. Ticketly generates tickets. Eventbrite has a marketplace. Here is exactly why none of them solve your problem.

Ticketly.pk

Built for Concerts, Not You

Ticketly lists Hassan Raheem and Atif Aslam concerts. To use it, you need their approval. You need a commercially viable public event. A university dinner or a coding bootcamp enrollment? You're not getting listed. It's a marketplace — not a tool for you to create your own payment-gated registration.

Jotform

40 Gateways, Zero Local

Jotform has Stripe, PayPal, Square, Authorize.net. None of these are used by 60% of Pakistan's online population. Having 40 wrong gateways is worse than 4 right ones. Plus: $34/month minimum, payment is optional (not enforced), steep learning curve, and single admin access on lower tiers.

Eventbrite

Takes 16% of Your Revenue

Selling 100 tickets at PKR 5,000? Eventbrite's cut is ~PKR 38,000. That's 16%+ of your gross revenue — gone before you've spent a rupee on the event itself. No JazzCash. No Easypaisa. Built for New York events, priced for New York wallets.
