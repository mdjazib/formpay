The Problem

GOOGLE FORMS
BROKE THE FLOW

Every event organizer, educator, small business owner, and NGO faces the same painful cycle: share a Google Form → collect registrations → chase payments manually → match receipts → follow up on ghosts. The payment step is completely disconnected from the data collection step.

😤

Manual Payment Chasing

Organizers share bank details separately, then spend hours cross-referencing screenshots, WhatsApp messages, and payment proofs with registration lists.

👻

Ghost Registrations

People register but never pay. Seats get blocked. Real attendees miss out. No-shows cost event organizers 20–35% of expected revenue.

🔀

Fragmented Tools

Current workarounds: Google Form + WhatsApp + bank transfer + manual Excel tracking. 4 tools to do 1 job.

💡

The Insight

Payment should be a required field. If you haven't paid, your form doesn't submit. No exceptions. No follow-ups. No lost revenue.

Acquisition Strategy

The go-to-market strategy is community-led, not ad-heavy. Target Pakistan's 800,000+ active Facebook event/organizer groups, WhatsApp business communities, and university societies. Partner with 10 influential event organizers in Lahore/Karachi as pilot users, offer them the Pro plan free for 6 months in exchange for testimonials and referrals.

SEO targeting "Google Forms payment Pakistan," "online registration with payment Pakistan," and similar high-intent low-competition keywords. Target ZERO paid ads in Year 1 — grow through organic community + word of mouth.

THE THESIS

Google Forms has 1 billion+ users. None of them can natively collect verified payments. Jotform exists but charges $34/month and doesn't support JazzCash or Easypaisa. FormPay captures the unserved South Asian SME, educator, and event organizer market with a product built specifically for their payment reality — not bolted onto a Western-first tool.

Market Opportunity

$696M market growing at 9.8% CAGR. South Asia (Pakistan + India) represents 500M+ internet users with growing mobile payment adoption — yet zero dedicated form-payment tool exists.

Defensible Moat

Local gateway integrations take months to build. Once FormPay has JazzCash + Easypaisa + Razorpay certified partnerships, this becomes a hard-to-replicate advantage.

Break-Even: 18 Months

With just 400 paid users at $9/month + transaction fees, FormPay reaches profitability. This is an achievable milestone from a tight, focused go-to-market in Pakistan alone.

Exit Potential

Strategic acqui-hires from Jotform, Typeform, or Stripe are plausible as they seek South Asian market entry. A 10x revenue multiple on $1.7M ARR (Year 4) = $17M exit.
